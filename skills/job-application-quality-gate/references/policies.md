# Policies

## Application Gates

Hard block when:

- Sponsorship is required and unavailable or unknown.
- Role requires local work authorization the tenant does not hold, with no sponsorship route.
- Remote role excludes the tenant's country or contracting setup.
- Form requires false attestation.
- Form asks for passport, government ID, immigration documents, exact current compensation, or references without explicit review.

## Approval

Standing consent is not enough for:

- CAPTCHA solving
- Final submit
- Email send
- Legal attestations
- Sensitive document uploads

Approval must refer to the current visible challenge/action or the specific recipient/destination.

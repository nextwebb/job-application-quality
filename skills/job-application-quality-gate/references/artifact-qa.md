# Artifact QA

Check every CV, resume, cover letter, and recruiter email for:

- Correct candidate identity and links.
- Correct current location.
- Correct company and role title.
- No placeholders.
- No unsupported claims.
- No invented numbers.
- No weak generic summary.
- Seniority signal appropriate to target role.
- Role-specific selected impact.
- Clean file naming and manifest hashes.

Do not upload or send artifacts that fail QA.

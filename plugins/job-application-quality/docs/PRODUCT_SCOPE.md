# Product Scope

Job Application Quality prepares and audits application packets.

## In Scope

- Candidate fact validation
- Role intake validation
- Sponsorship and location gating
- CV/cover-letter packet preparation
- Recruiter email drafts
- Artifact manifests and QA reports
- Submission checklists and logs

## Out of Scope

- Inventing claims or metrics
- CAPTCHA bypass
- Automated mass submission
- Legal or immigration advice
- Scraping protected sites
- Sending email without explicit approval
- Clicking final submit without explicit approval

## Principle

The agent can do analytical and clerical work. The human owns truth, authorization, and final submission intent.
